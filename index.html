What Obscurum is (and isn’t)

Most businesses only discover cybercrime activity after it’s already expensive: chargebacks, account takeovers, customer phishing, brand damage, or an insurance/legal mess. Obscurum gives you early warning and clear response steps.

What it is

External cybercrime visibility: monitoring for leaks, impersonation, phishing, and fraud signals connected to your brand.

What it’s not

Not penetration testing. Not a SOC. Not “we’ll secure everything.” It’s targeted intelligence + action.

Who it’s for

E-commerce, SaaS, online platforms, and service businesses that rely on reputation, accounts, and customer trust.

FAQ

The questions founders ask before they buy (and the straight answers).

Is this legal?

Yes. Obscurum uses lawful OSINT and threat intelligence methods. We do not hack systems, infiltrate groups, or purchase stolen data.

Can you remove stolen data from the dark web?

No one can reliably erase criminal copies. What we do is reduce impact: resets, MFA enforcement, access revocation, and takedowns where feasible (impersonation/phishing).

What do you need from us?

Usually: your domains, email patterns (e.g., @brand.com), brand terms, and a point of contact. The base offerings typically do not require admin access.

What if you find nothing?

That’s still useful. You’ll get a report documenting what was checked and the top controls to keep exposure low.

How fast are results?

Starter is typically 3–5 days. Exposure Sprint is typically within 7 days for most SMBs.

Will you work with our IT provider/MSP?

Yes. We can hand off remediation steps and coordinate on takedown requests and internal control improvements.
